Privacy Policy for OSS Etiquette v1.0 Adopters


Effective Date: December 12, 2024

Privacy Policy outlines how we handle personal data when contributors submit pull requests to have their projects or organizations listed on the OSS Etiquette v1.0 Adopters page, hosted on Cloudflare Pages.

1. Information We Collect

By submitting a pull request to our GitHub repository, you are voluntarily providing your GitHub Username, which is used for attribution and communication. Project or Organization Name to be displayed on the Adopters page. Repository or Website Link to direct visitors to the adopterâ€™s project. Contribution Message or Description as part of the pull request.

2. How We Use This Information

The information that you submit will be used to display your project or organization on the OSS Etiquette v1.0 "Adopters" webpage. Provide attribution by linking to your GitHub profile or project. Maintain transparency and acknowledgment for contributions.

3. Public Display of Information

All information submitted via pull requests is publicly visible on GitHub and the OSS Etiquette Adopters page hosted on Cloudflare Pages. By submitting a pull request, you consent to this public display.

4. Your Control Over Your Data

You retain full control over the data you submit. You can request the removal of your project or organization from the Adopters page. Modify your submission by updating your pull request or contacting us directly.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

The information that you submit will only be displayed on the OSS Etiquette Adopters page (hosted on Cloudflare Pages) and on GitHub as part of the contribution process. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

Your contribution will remain publicly displayed until you submit a request for removal or you update your information via a pull request.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your submitted data: Access in order to review the information displayed on the Adopters page. Modification whenever you submit a pull request or contact us to update your listing. Removal, you can also request removal of your listing by contacting us or submitting a pull request.
To exercise your rights, contact us at [your email address].

8. Consent

By submitting a pull request to the OSS Etiquette repository, you consent to publicly display your GitHub username, project or organization name and related links. The handling of your information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your submitted data, please contact us via github.com/KERNAMON

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on the OSS Etiquette repository and website. The updated policy will include a new effective date.




By following these guidelines, we aim to create a transparent and respectful environment for all contributors. Thank you for being a part of the OSS Etiquette community!
